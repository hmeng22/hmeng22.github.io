Regular Expression

圆括号 "()" 分组
方括号 "[]" 匹配字符
花括号 "{}" 匹配字符的数量

插入符 "^" 开始
美元符 "$" 结束

.		除换行符以外任意单字符
a|b		a or b
*		0 or more times
+		at least 1 time
?		0 or 1 time
{n}		more than n times
{n, m}	between n and m times
Reg
\d [0-9] Digit
\D [^0-9] Non-digit
\s [\s\t\r\n\f] Whitespace
\S [^\s\t\r\n\f] Non-Whitespace
\w [A-Za-z0-9] Word
\W [^A-Za-z0-9] Non-word
